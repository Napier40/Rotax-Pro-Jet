Scientific Approach

Our jetting calculator is based on the mathematical model developed by Bowmain Consulting for Rotax carburetor jetting. The core formula for jet size calculation is:

j₂ = j₁ × (ρ₂/ρ₁)^(1/4)

Where:

  • j₂ is the new jet size
  • j₁ is the reference jet size
  • ρ₂ is the current air density
  • ρ₁ is the reference air density

Air density is calculated using temperature, pressure, and humidity data to ensure the most accurate recommendations possible.

Air Density Formula

ρ = (P / (Rd × T)) × (1 - 0.378 × (Pw/P))

Where:

  • ρ = air density (kg/m³)
  • P = atmospheric pressure (Pascals)
  • Pw = water vapor pressure (Pascals)
  • Rd = gas constant for dry air (287.05 J/(kg*K))
  • T = temperature (Kelvin)
