About Rotax Pro Jet

We're a team of kart racing enthusiasts and engineers dedicated to helping racers achieve optimal engine performance through precise carburetor jetting.

Our Story

Rotax Pro Jet was born from the frustration of inconsistent engine performance due to changing weather conditions. As kart racers ourselves, we experienced firsthand the challenges of adjusting carburetor jetting to match the environment.

After countless hours of research, testing, and collaboration with engine tuners, we developed a mathematical model based on the work of Bowmain Consulting that accurately predicts the optimal jetting for any given atmospheric conditions.

What started as a simple spreadsheet for our own team has evolved into a comprehensive web application trusted by kart racers around the world. Our mission is to take the guesswork out of jetting and help racers of all levels achieve consistent, optimal engine performance.
